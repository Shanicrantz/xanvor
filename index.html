Two letters,

XANVOR draws its identity from the heart of its own name — the letters N and V, drawn as a single inseparable form. Where one stroke ends, the next begins. One gesture doing the work of two.

The Binding is a bespoke glyph. It exists in no font, no catalog, no archive. It belongs only to XANVOR.

That is the promise.
