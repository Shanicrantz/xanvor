The Workshop · Four Steps

From design to your shelf

  1. 01

    Design & CAD

    Your reference becomes a signed-off CAD file. Tooling begins only after you approve.

  2. 02

    Cast & shape

    Sand-cast in brass, copper or aluminium at our Moradabad foundry, then turned and shaped to spec.

  3. 03

    Hand-finish

    Fettled, hammered, engraved, polished, antiqued or gilt — by craftspeople trained in the local tradition.

  4. 04

    Pack & ship

    Plain export carton or your branded packaging — sea ex Mundra / Nhava Sheva, air for sample runs, DDP available.
