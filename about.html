About

Metal handicrafts, hand-finished in Moradabad

XANVOR · a house brand of Zenko Inc.

XANVOR is the metal-handicrafts house brand of Zenko Inc., a partnership firm based in Moradabad — the city that has shaped brass for generations. We design, hand-finish and assemble brass, copper, silver-gilt and sheesham décor under one roof, and carry each piece outward through export and our own retail store.

Every XANVOR piece is hand-hammered and hand-finished by artisans in our Moradabad workshop. Small marks of the maker — a hammer line, a turn in the wood grain, a subtle shift in patina — are part of the character of a handmade object, not a defect.

What we make

Our collections span hot-serve food warmers, casseroles and hot-pots; artisanal serving trays; copper home and tableware; decorative boxes, canisters and vanity pieces; and gifting-ready sets in brass and copper. From everyday serveware to wedding, festive and corporate gifting — each is built to be used and kept.
